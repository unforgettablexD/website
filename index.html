What I am trying to prove

I am not trying to present myself as a pure-theory applicant. My path is more direct: build something real, stress-test it, write down what failed, and extract a cleaner research question.

The main story is simple: I want to turn trading and market experimentation into a serious research program around reinforcement learning, execution-aware decision-making, and stochastic modeling.
